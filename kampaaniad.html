The Biggest Video Game Companies

Sony Computer Entertainment

Founded by Ken Kutaragi and Norio Ohga on 16th November 1993 in Japan
Examples: Bloodborne, Ghost Of Tsushima, Sackboy: A Big Adventure

Xbox Game Studios (Microsoft Studios)

Founded by Microsoft in March 2000 in the US
Examples: Forza Motorsport, Minecraft, Ori And The Blind Forest

Nintendo

Founded by Fusajiro Yamauchi on 23th September 1889 in Japan
Examples: Kirby, The Legend Of Zelda, Animal Crossing: New Horizons

Sega

Founded by Martin Bromley and Richard Stewart on 3rd June 1960 in Japan
Examples: Super Monkey Ball, Team Sonic Racing, Yakuza: Like A Dragon

Activision Blizzard

Founded by Bobby Kotick on 10th July 2008 in the US
Examples: Doom, Vampire The Masquerade - Bloodlines, Prototype

Namco Bandai

Founded by Masaya Nakamura on 31th March 2006 in Japan
Examples: Splatterhouse, Code Vein, Tekken 7

Electronic Arts

Founded by Trip Hawkins on 28th May 1982 in the US
Examples: Mirror's Edge, Titanfall, Anthem

Ubisoft

Founded by Christian Guillemot, Claude Guillemot, Gérard Guillemot, Michel Guillemot
and Yves Guillemot on 28th March 1986 in France
Examples: Prince Of Persia: The Sands Of Time, The Crew, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six: Siege

Konami

Founded by Kagemasa Kōzuki on 21th March 1969 in Japan
Examples: Castlevania, Silent Hill 2, Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain

Square Enix

Founded by Yasuhiro Fukushima and Masafumi Miyamoto on 22th September 1975 in Japan
Examples: Final Fantasy, Nier, Avengers
