A Little About Video Games' History 🕹️

A video game is an electronic game that involves interaction with a user interface or
input device to generate visual feedback for a player.

Then it is shown on a 2D or 3D video display device or virtual reality headset.
Video games are augmented with audio feedback and optionally with other types of feedback systems.
Video games are defined based on their platform - arcade games, console games and PC games.
More recently, the industry has expanded onto mobile gaming.

Video games are classified into a wide range of genres based on their type of gameplay and purpose.

First Video Game And It's Developer

The world’s first video game is Tennis For Two
It was the first computer game to be created purely as an entertainment product
rather than for academic research or commercial technology promotion

It was created by physicist William Higinbotham and shown on October 18, 1958.
